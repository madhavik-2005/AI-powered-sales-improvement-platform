Sales Objection Handling:
- Acknowledge customer concerns before responding
- Ask clarifying questions instead of assuming objections
- Provide social proof when addressing price objections

Discovery Questions:
- Ask open-ended questions to understand customer pain points
- Let the customer speak more than the sales representative
- Summarize customer needs to confirm understanding

Closing Techniques:
- Clearly summarize value before asking for commitment
- Use soft closes like proposing a demo or pilot
- Address remaining objections before closing

Tone and Empathy Best Practices:
- Maintain a calm and respectful tone
- Avoid interrupting the customer
- Adapt communication style to match the customer

Follow-Up Strategies:
- Clearly define next steps at the end of the call
- Send a follow-up message summarizing key points
- Schedule the next interaction while interest is high
